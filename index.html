Trip Over This!

For when you have to go somewhere you don't know, you don't want to be there, and you want to find some things to distract you from your misery. Do you have to travel to an all-day accounting meeting that your boss sent you to because she didn't want to go? Are you in an unfamiliar town becuase your uncle is marrying a stripper half his age? Just use this app to find something to help you check out or relieve your frustrations. Heck, maybe just skip the meeting or the wedding altogether and just do the fun stuff you find here, because who really wants to deal with boredom or drama?
